Tom Brady

Biography

Tom Brady was born August 3, 1977 in San Mateo, California. He spent his college years at Michigan until he was drafted in 2000 by the New England Patriots. On September 24, 2001 Tom Brady became the starting Quarterback for the Patriots, a position he has held to this day.

Professional Highlights
